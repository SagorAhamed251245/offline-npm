## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Consistency > Intensity**
   - Better to post weekly than burn out
   - Small, regular updates win

2. **Engage with Your Users**
   - Respond to issues quickly
   - Accept pull requests
   - Build community

3. **Update Regularly**
   - Even small updates refresh your listing
   - Announce each update on social media

4. **Leverage User Feedback**
   - Ask users what features they want
   - Implement popular requests
   - Credit contributors

5. **Cross-Promote**
   - Link to your package in all content
   - Use consistent branding
   - Make it easy to find
